Recruitment / Application Process

1. Fill-out the application form.

2. The team will contact you through email in about 1 week after the submission. We will notify you about whether the first round of selection was passed.

3. If passing the selection, we will set up a small 10-min interview with you to discuss briefly about your skills, visions, and goals.

4. The final result will be sent through email 1-2 weeks after the interview.
